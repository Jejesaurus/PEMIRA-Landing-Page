Welcome to PEMIRA FASILKOM 2026 ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our voting platform at Pemira Fasilkom UPNVJT 2026 Website. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google Authentication, we collect the following information from your Google account:

  • Full Name — to identify you as a registered voter
  • Email Address — to verify your eligibility and send notifications
  • Profile Photo — to display your identity within the platform
  • Student ID Number (NIM) / Campus Data — to confirm your status as an active student of Fakultas Ilmu Komputer

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Verifying your eligibility to participate in the election
  • Ensuring each eligible voter can only cast one vote
  • Displaying your profile within the voting platform
  • Maintaining the integrity and security of the election process
  • Communicating important election-related information to you

3. Google OAuth & Third-Party Access

We use Google OAuth 2.0 to authenticate users. When you sign in with Google, you are granting PEMIRA FASILKOM 2026 limited access to your Google account information as described above. We do not have access to your Google password or any data beyond what is explicitly requested during sign-in.

Google's use of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized administrators of PEMIRA FASILKOM 2026. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

5. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained only for the duration of the PEMIRA FASILKOM 2026 election period. After the election concludes and results are officially announced, all personal data collected through this platform will be permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to election integrity requirements)
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended exclusively for active students of Fakultas Ilmu Komputer. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 17.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
